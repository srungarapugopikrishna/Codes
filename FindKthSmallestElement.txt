Given two sorted arrays Array1[],Array2[] find the kth smallest element from them.
Example.
Function def:
-------------
int kthSmallestElement(int Array1[],int Array2[],int k){}

Sample I/p and O/p:
i/p 1:
 a1[]={1,3,5,7}
 a2[]={2,4,6,8,10}
 k=5;
o/p=5
-------------
i/p 2:
 a1[]={1,2,3}
 a2[]={2,3,4,5}
 k=4;
o/p=3
------------
i/p 3:
 a1[]={2,4,12,15}
 a2[]={3,4,13,15}
 k=6;
o/p=13
